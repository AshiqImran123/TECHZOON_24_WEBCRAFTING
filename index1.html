Healthcare Chatbot FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

We offer a wide range of healthcare services, including general consultations, specialist consultations, mental health services, and wellness advice.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment directly through our chatbot by specifying your needs, or you can call our service center.

Is my health information secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and security. All personal and health information is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with healthcare regulations.

Can I get a prescription through this service?

Yes, based on the consultation with our healthcare professionals, we can issue prescriptions that you can use at your local pharmacy.
